About THE BIO HACKATHON 2024

THE BIO HACKATHON

The competition is a groundbreaking initiative that merges the power of artificial intelligence with critical sectors like agriculture, education, and medicine.

By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, the competition aims to develop cutting-edge technologies that can be implemented in today's world, driving sustainable growth, improving learning outcomes, and providing personalized medical care.

Participants will not only showcase their technical prowess but also contribute to a better future by solving real-world problems.

F.A.Q

  • What is the goal of the competition?

    The competition aims to merge artificial intelligence with key sectors such as agriculture, education, and healthcare to develop innovative solutions for real-world problems.

  • Who can participate in the competition?

    The competition is open only for undergraduate student from universities across Mauritius.

  • How can I register for the competition?

    You can register for the competition by visiting our official website and filling out the registration form available under the "Register" section.

  • Is there a registration fee?

    No, there is no registration fee. Participation in the competition is completely free.

  • What are the key dates to remember?

    Key dates include the registration deadline, the announcement of shortlisted participants, the competition dates, and the award ceremony. Please check our official website for specific dates.

  • Will there be any prizes for the winners?

    Yes, there will be exciting prizes for the top-performing teams, including cash awards, certificates, and opportunities for further development and mentorship.
