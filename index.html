About THE BIO TECH HACKATHON 2024

THE BIO TECH HACKATHON

The competition is a groundbreaking initiative that merges the power of artificial intelligence with critical sectors like agriculture, education, and medicine.

By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, the competition aims to develop cutting-edge technologies that can be implemented in today's world, driving sustainable growth, improving learning outcomes, and providing personalized medical care.

Participants will not only showcase their technical prowess but also contribute to a better future by solving real-world problems.

F.A.Q

  • 1. What is the goal of the competition?

    The competition aims to merge artificial intelligence with key sectors such as agriculture, education, and healthcare to develop innovative solutions for real-world problems.

  • 2. Who can participate in the competition?

    The competition is open only for undergraduate student from universities across Mauritius.

  • 3. How can I register for the competition?

    You can register for the competition by visiting our official website and filling out the registration form available under the "Register Now" section.

  • 4. Is there a registration fee?

    No, there is no registration fee. Participation in the competition is completely free.

  • 5. Will there be any prizes for the winners?

    Yes, there will be exciting prizes for the top-performing teams, including cash awards, certificates, and opportunities for networking.

  • 6. Can we participate as a team?

    Yes, participants can form teams of three to four members. Collaboration is encouraged to bring diverse skills and perspectives to the projects.

  • 7. What type of application is expected at the end of the competition

    Your application can be developed on any platform using any tech you want but is should be either a web applciation or a mobile application.

  • 8. Where should you submit your work

    Teams should push their repositories on github with a proper README file with screenshots of project. Repositories should be made public

  • 9. Should participants host their websites

    Yes. pariticipants should host their website on NETLIFY and provide us with the links. For mobile application, a final apk shall be included in the repositories

  • 10. How selection process will take place

    After receiving all the proposal, only top 10 teams with most promising solution/ideas will be selected and their team leader will be mailed.
